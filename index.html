About

Hello, I'm Owen Innes the host of The Scottish History Podcast.

I have been interested in History all my life and in 2016 I became a tour guide with a local company, here in Edinburgh.

I then guided for 2 and a half years before moving on to other things. However my passion for history never left me, and being an amateur musician, I had all the equipment just sitting around to record a podcast. And so, here we are!

Welcome to The Scottish History Podcast.

You can contact me directly through any of the social media channels or on the dedicated e-mail address which you will find below in the Contact section.
